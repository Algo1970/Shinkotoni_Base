Pizza Hut

2021.04

The framework of the pizza hut was made of 90mm square cypress wood.


The roofing material was made of Ondurin.


First, we put down gravel and hardened it with a homemade tamper.


Making the mortar foundation for the first time was hard work.


next, we stacked about 250 heat-resistant bricks.


The pizza oven itself was purchased online, and as it weighs about 160 kg, it required the whole family to set it up.


We baked pizza 7-8 times this year. We heated the pizza oven to 400 degrees and baked each pizza in less than 2 minutes. We made the sauce with tomatoes from the garden and topped it with basil from the garden, so we were able to bake the most delicious pizza with that.
